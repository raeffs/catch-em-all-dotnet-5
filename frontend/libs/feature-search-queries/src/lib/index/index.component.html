Not sure what to do here? No problem, we've got you covered!

Collect & Minimize Expenses

You can start by searching for some precious treasures that you would like to collect. Maybe you want to start a new collection? Or you already collected a ton of stuff, but there is this one piece that's still missing? The app will crawl through the depths of the internet and notify you once it found it.

Sell & Maximize Profit

You don't care about collecting things? Maybe you have something laying around that you'd like to sell, but you don't know at which price? In that case the app can keep track of others that try to sell the same stuff as you and tell you at which price someone actually bought it.

And if you're not sure what you're looking for; don't worry. Just give it a try! You can adjust everything later or start over from scratch if you don't like the result.
